Bagel Grilled Cheese

  • Prep time: 5 mins
  • Cook time: 5 mins
  • Total time: 10 mins
  • Servings: 1

This everything bagel grilled cheese sandwich is taken to another level just by starting with an everything-seasoned bagel. It's perfectly crunchy on the outside, and really, any melty cheese will work.


Ingredients

  • 1 everything bagel, sliced in half horizontally
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • 1 slice American cheese
  • 1 slice Provolone cheese

Directions

Step 1

Preheat a skillet over medium heat.


Step 2

Slather 1/2 tablespoon mayonnaise on each cut side of bagel. Place 1/2 bagel, mayonnaise side, down in skillet. Top with both slices of cheese and other bagel half, cut side up.


Step 3

Press down on sandwich with spatula, and cook until golden brown, 2 to 4 minutes. Flip sandwich over, press down, and cook until browned, 2 to 4 minutes more.
